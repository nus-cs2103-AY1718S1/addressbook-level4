Welcome to your TunedIn Application Transfer Process.

This guide serves as an aid for the transfer of your application data. Should you encounter any problems, please feel free to email us at help@tunedinapp.com

1. Install your new working copy of TunedIn on your new computer.
2. Copy the files preferences.json and config.json, and replace them in the TunedIn folder.
3. Delete the folder 'data', and replace it with the 'data' folder found in the ZIP file.
